Customer Concerns for SMUSH
sheet music upload service helper

Reliability
	Storage of music data should be reliable and resilient...fire,
	tornadoes etc...
Ability to add new music
	Need to be able to add new music to the archives/database
	Needs to be controlled and monitored so music in the archives/db is accurate and reliable
	Music should be verifiable by person if necessary, original image/pdf still accessible

Availability
	Music should be readily available for users who wish to access it

Accessibility
	Multiple ways to add music to the archives, able to add in different formats

Searchability
	Customer wants a simple way to search archives for music
